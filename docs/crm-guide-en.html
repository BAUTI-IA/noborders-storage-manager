HOW TO · Move a job to its next stage

  1. Find the job's row (use the search box or the sub-tabs).
  2. In Actions, tap the advance button — it always names the next stage, e.g. → In storage.
  3. The status flow is: Scheduled → Picked up → In storage → Out for delivery → Delivered. Only Full jobs pass through In storage; Direct and Broker jobs skip it. Marking Delivered removes the job from the active lists.
Other row actions: 🗺️ Ruta (Route) opens Google Maps storage → customer · 💬 WhatsApp opens WhatsApp with the full job info pre-written (or the trip manifest if it rides on a trip) · 🗑 Delete deletes the job (section 19 warning applies).

3Creating a job

The form adapts to the job type. Below: the modal as you'll see it, then every field explained.

HOW TO · Create a new job

  1. Tap + New job (top right in Dispatching, Jobs or the calendars).
  2. Fill Basic info: Job # (required — if it already exists you'll see a ⚠️ duplicate warning), Job type (Full / Direct / Broker), Broker, Client (required) + Client phone / Client email, Rep, Status, Calendar status (color) and tick one or more Drivers.
  3. Fill the Pick up block (Full/Direct): date from/to (a range is allowed), address, city, state, zip. Full jobs also have Extra stops.
  4. Fill the Delivery block: FADD (required!), delivery date, address, city, state, zip.
  5. Fill Load / Carga: Volumen (CF) — estimado broker (broker's estimated volume), Real CF (medido al cargar) (real measured CF — leave empty to use the estimate), Sticker color, Lot number, notes.
  6. Pads: received from broker / returned — "missing" is computed automatically.
  7. Fill Financiero (Financials): Estimate, Deposit, Pickup balance, Delivery balance, Precio/CF, Fuel surcharge %. Broker deliveries instead show: BOL balance to collect from client, Carrier rate/CF, auto Carrier total, Closing sheet link, BOL collected. Optional: tick "🤝 El broker se queda con un % del balance" (broker keeps a % of the balance) and set the %.
  8. Open Storage (opcional) and tick where it's stored: a 🏭 warehouse (Indiana / New Jersey) and/or one or more rented units; set Date in.
  9. (Full jobs) Open Client storage billing and tick "Bill this client for storage (every 30 days)" if they pay monthly — see section 7.
  10. Tap Save job. Done — the job appears on Dispatching instantly for everyone.

HOW TO · Edit a job field on the spot

  1. Open the job (tap its Job # anywhere).
  2. In Job data, click the value you want to change (FADD, client, addresses, balances, sticker, Real CF…). It turns into an input.
  3. Type the new value and press Enter (dates save when you pick them). For the full form use the Edit button.

HOW TO · Log an event in the job's timeline

  1. In the job detail, scroll to Timeline and tap + Add evento (add event).
  2. Set the Date and pick the Event type: Picked up · Loaded to truck · Dropped at storage · Loaded from storage to truck · Delivered · On hold · Attempted delivery — not home · Redispatched · Driver handoff · Other.
  3. Optionally pick the Storage / warehouse and Trip # it relates to, add Notes, and tap Save event.
  4. Trip actions (drops, deliveries) appear in the timeline automatically with an "auto" tag — you only log what the system can't see.
Where it's stored: the last section shows each storage location of the job with its gate code (one-tap copy), ✂️ Split (divide between two trucks) and Mark delivered per portion.

HOW TO · Schedule a delivery

  1. Open Delivery Calendar. The 📋 Entregas por agendar panel lists every job that's ready but has no delivery date; jobs with FADD ≤ 7 days are flagged in red.
  2. On the job's row, pick the date and tap Agendar (schedule). The job lands on the calendar.

HOW TO · Put a job on a specific day

  1. Click a day header (it shows a "+") → choose 🆕 Create new job on this day or 📋 Add an existing job to this day.
  2. For an existing job: search by job # / client / driver — only jobs without a pickup (or delivery) date are listed — then tap Add.
  3. Toggle Week / Month and navigate with ← Today →. Event colors follow the legend: Active · On hold/Redispatch · Cancelled · Long haul · Delivered.

6Storage — units & warehouses

Tabs: Storage Units (rented lockers) · 🏭 Indiana · 🏭 New Jersey (own warehouses). Top buttons: Importar WhatsApp · + Unit · + Job a unidad (add job to a unit).

HOW TO · Add a rented storage unit

  1. Tap + Unit. Fill: Company (pick or type — CubeSmart…), Status (state, e.g. TN), Zip, Address, Unit # (duplicates are flagged ⚠️), Tamaño (size, e.g. 10x10), Gate Code, Lock/Combo, Driver que abre la unit (driver who opens it), Email / Account # / Phone, Monthly cost, card, Open date, Payment due date.
  2. Tap Save. In the list, set the CF capacity with Set capacity so the occupancy bar works.
  3. The gate code copies with one click from the table. Renew — add 30 days (in the unit detail) extends the payment due date.

HOW TO · Import units from WhatsApp

  1. Tap Importar WhatsApp. Choose Pegar texto (paste text) or Subir ZIP del chat (upload the exported chat .zip).
  2. Paste the group messages (or drop the .zip), tap Previsualizar (preview) — the system detects "{driver} · {company} · Unit {unit}" entries.
  3. Untick any you don't want and tap Importar (n).

7Storage Billing — monthly charges to clients

For clients who pay a monthly fee to keep their goods stored. The system generates each 30-day charge by itself.

HOW TO · Activate billing for a client

  1. Either tick "Bill this client for storage (every 30 days)" inside the job form, or go to Storage Billing and tap + Add billing.
  2. In the modal: search the Job, set the Monthly rate ($), the Billing start date, tick First month free if agreed, add Notes, and tap Activate billing.
  3. From then on the system creates each period's charge automatically and flips late ones to Overdue.

HOW TO · Collect & remind

  1. Open Storage Billing. Metrics on top: Active storage clients · Outstanding this month · Overdue · Due this week · Collected this month; tabs All / Pending / Overdue / Paid.
  2. Each client card shows the rate, the current period and the amount due. Tap Mark as paid when the money arrives.
  3. Tap 💬 Send reminder to open WhatsApp with a polite payment reminder pre-written (it never reveals where the goods are stored). Edit rate changes the monthly amount.

8Brokers

The list of companies we work for — Allied, Atlas, Mayflower, United, North American, Wheaton, Arpin, Bekins, plus "Direct (no broker)". Each row shows contact info, how many jobs and the pending balance. Add, edit or delete from here; job forms pick brokers from this list.

9Settlements — broker closing sheets

Where broker-delivery money gets reconciled. Metrics: Open closing sheets · Broker owes us · We owe brokers · Outstanding BOL collections · Pads outstanding. Tabs: Open / Settled / Disputed / All.

HOW TO · Create a closing sheet and link jobs

  1. Tap + Closing sheet. Fill: CS number (e.g. CS-1024), Load date, Broker, Driver, Status. Optionally upload the original document (photo/PDF).
  2. In "Jobs en este closing sheet" search jobs by # or client and tick them (jobs already on another sheet are flagged).
  3. Set the Missing pad charge ($) (default 7) and the broker deductions: Trip cost, Labor, Other fees + description.
  4. Tap Create. You can also link from the job detail → "Carrier Settlement" → + Add to closing sheet… or ➕ Create new.

HOW TO · Record a BOL collection

  1. Open the sheet (tap Ver (view)) and find the job's row → tap Record payment.
  2. Enter Amount collected ($), Collection date, Payment method and notes → Save collection.
  3. ⚡ The collection syncs automatically with Payments — never enter it twice.
  4. The detail shows the whole math: carrier fees − deductions − pads = "Broker owes you $X" (or "You owe the broker"). When settled, tap Mark settled. Use 📄 Export PDF or 💬 WhatsApp broker to send it.

10Extras & Commissions

Extras are additional charges on a job. The system computes each driver's and rep's commission automatically.

HOW TO · Add an extra to a job

  1. Open the job detail → Extras → + Add extra (or from the Extras page).
  2. Pick the Type: Extra CF · Shuttle · Long carry · Stairs · Packing · Flight charge · Other.
  3. Enter the Amount ($). For Extra CF you instead enter CF count × Rate per CF (+ fuel surcharge %) and the total is computed.
  4. Set Generated by: Driver only / Driver + Rep / Rep only (Long carry and Stairs are always driver-only), pick the Driver (pre-filled from the job) and the Rep if any.
  5. The Driver % / Rep % pre-fill from the rules below — edit if needed. The summary shows each commission and the company's net. Tap Save extra.

HOW TO · Pay out commissions each month

  1. Open Extras. Metrics: Extras (month) · Driver commissions · Rep commissions · For the company. Filter by driver, rep, month or type.
  2. Use the 🧑‍✈️ Drivers tab (grouped by driver, collapsible by month) or 👤 Reps / Back office. Each group ends in a TOTAL row and a highlighted COMMISSION row.
  3. Tap 📋 Copiar (copy) or 🖨️ PDF to send each person their summary.
  4. Manage reps with Reps / Employees (top bar): add name/role/phone/email; tap a rep's name for their profile with commission per month and full history.
⚠️ "Extras collected via payment with no commission assigned": if a payment was recorded against an extra but nobody assigned the commission, a banner lists them — tap Assign commission on each to fix the split.

11Trips / Live Load — trucks on the road

A trip = one truck + one driver + an ordered list of stops. View tabs: 🗺️ Live map · Active trips · Unassigned trips · All trips. Statuses: Loading → In transit → Completed (or Cancelled).

HOW TO · Create a trip

  1. Tap + Trip. Set Trip number (TRIP-001…), Departure date, Truck (options show capacity: "Truck 1 · 1,600 CF"), Driver. New trips start as Loading.
  2. In "Stops — delivery order", search jobs by # / client / storage and tick to add. The capacity bar updates live ("{n} CF left").
  3. Reorder stops with ↑ / ↓. Toggle a stop between 🚚 Delivery and 🔁 Relocation (internal move, no delivery, no collection).
  4. Tap Create trip, then on the card 💬 Send manifest to driver (WhatsApp with the whole stop list) and Depart when the truck leaves.

HOW TO · Drop a job at a storage mid-trip

  1. On the stop, tap 📦 Dropped at storage.
  2. Choose the destination unit or 🏭 warehouse. Unit not listed? Tap "+ Unit not on the list? Create it", fill Company / Unit # / State / size and Create & select.
  3. Tap Confirm drop. The job goes back to storage and stays on the trip faded out as "📦 Dropped in storage".

HOW TO · Split a job between two trucks

  1. Tap ✂️ Split on the stop (or in the job detail's "Where it's stored").
  2. Set how many CF go to each part and pick the destination trip/truck for the second part.
  3. Both portions track separately; the job detail shows each portion and offers ✂️ Merge portions to undo.

HOW TO · Add a custom (non-job) stop

  1. Tap ➕ Add stop on the trip card.
  2. Pick a Category: 🔧 Maintenance · 🛠️ Repair · 🔍 DOT inspection · ⚖️ Weigh station · ⛽ Fuel · 🛌 Rest (DOT hours) · 🅿️ Overnight/parking · 🚚 Equipment · 🏢 Office/terminal · 📋 Other, plus optional address and note.
  3. Custom stops live in the same ordered list — edit them with ✏️, tick them done with ✅.

HOW TO · Use the Route popup & AI suggestions

  1. 🗺️ View route opens a US map with numbered pins and the stop list: tick which stops to include, reorder with ▲▼ (saved per trip), then Open in Google Maps.
  2. ✨ Suggest trips (AI) (top bar) analyzes pending jobs and trucks and proposes groupings — each card shows occupancy, the job order and "💡 Why this trip". Tap Review & create to accept or Dismiss.
  3. Mid-trip, the trip detail also offers ➕ Add job, 🔼 Load from storage, 🆕 Unplanned pickup and 🔄 Handoff — hand the whole trip or single jobs to another driver, choosing the reason (better for this delivery / truck change / availability / other); everything is logged.
  4. When every stop is done you'll see "✅ All jobs delivered — mark trip as completed?" → tap Mark completed.
Also in Fleet: the 🗺️ Live map tab shows each truck's last known position (moving / stopped) — update it via "Update location". The Equipment section tracks pads, dollies, straps etc. per location with 🚚 Load on trip / 📤 Unload.

12Payments — every dollar, tracked 💰

Metrics: Expected this month · Received this month · In circulation (not deposited) · Deposited this month · Pending collection · CC fees collected. Tabs: All / Pending / Received / In circulation / Deposited.

HOW TO · Record a payment

  1. Tap + Payment (top bar, or from the job detail's Payments section — there it comes pre-filled with the outstanding balance and the job's driver).
  2. Job: search by job # or client and pick it. Set the Payment date and the Amount ($).
  3. Concept: Job / Extra / CC Fee / Storage / Other. Optional Discount ($) + reason. Payment stage: At pick up / At delivery / Other.
  4. Method (pill row): Cash · Credit card · Zelle · Venmo · Check · Money order · PayPal · Cash App · Wire transfer · Apple Pay.
  5. Checks ask for the type (Cashier's / Personal / Official) + serial number, bank, date, memo and a check photo. Money orders ask for the issuer (USPS / Western Union / MoneyGram / Other) + serial and details. Credit card can add a CC fee charged to the client (a separate CC-Fee payment is created).
  6. Tick Received (+ by whom and when — automatic for digital payments). For cash/checks/money orders set "Who has the money?" (pre-filled with the job's driver) and, once banked, Deposited + date + Bank account. Digital methods are auto-deposited.
  7. Tap Create payment.

HOW TO · Split a payment across charges (allocation)

  1. In the payment modal, tick "✂️ Asignar a cargos (split)" (allocate to charges).
  2. The purple panel lists the job's charges (job balance, each pending extra) with its Pendiente (pending) amount. Type how much of the payment goes to each — the Restante (remaining) per charge updates live; overpays are flagged but allowed.
  3. Anything you don't assign stays as "A cuenta" (on account) — you can allocate it later with the row's Asignar button.

14BOL & Documents — paperwork and signatures

Generate the Bill of Lading over the company's PDF template, stamp values where they belong, and get it signed by email through DocuSign.

HOW TO · Generate a BOL

  1. Open BOL → Generate BOL (or from a job: 📄 Generate BOL in the job detail footer).
  2. Pick the Company template and the Job (search by # or client) — the job's data loads and everything stays editable.
  3. Review Client & route (addresses, dates), tick the template's Checkboxes, and fill Charges: Base CF, Rate/CF, Fuel % — add lines with + Extra CF, + Charge, + Discount. The totals (Grand total, Balance due, Due at pickup, Due at delivery) update live in the preview.
  4. Need text in a specific spot? Tap ✎ Add text on PDF → + Add text → click on the PDF where it goes; drag to move, drag the corner to resize, double-click to edit. Then ✓ Done editing.
  5. Tap Save as final — it's filed in Documents. (Save draft keeps it editable; ⬇ Download for a copy.)

HOW TO · Get it signed (pickup, then delivery)

  1. Open Documents (BOL section). Files are organized in 📁 folders per broker; each row shows its signature status: Generated → Pickup sent → Pickup signed → Delivery sent → Delivery signed → Completed ✓.
  2. Tap Firmar pickup (sign pickup), enter the customer's email — they get a normal DocuSign email and sign from their phone. When they sign, the signed copy appears here by itself (badge PU ✍).
  3. At delivery time, tap Firmar delivery — same flow; the delivery is signed on the same document with the pickup signature still visible (badge DEL ✍).
  4. Need to change a signed BOL? Reopen it, edit, and save — it becomes a new version that inherits the pickup signature, ready to sign at delivery. Delivery signatures are never carried over.
Templates (admins): + New template → upload the company PDF, add fields with + Add field or let ✨ Auto-detect (AI) find them, map each to a job field / fixed text / checkbox / signature, then Save & activate.

15Claims & Incidents

Anything that goes wrong becomes a claim and is tracked to resolution. Jobs and trips with open claims show a ⚠ badge.

HOW TO · Report and follow up a claim

  1. Open Claims & Incidents → + New claim.
  2. Fill Incident data: link the Job (search) and Trip if any, Client, Incident date, Incident type (💥 Damage · 🚨 Theft · 🔍 Missing items · 📭 Incomplete delivery · 😠 Complaint · 📝 Other), Assigned to (who follows it up) and the Description.
  3. Set Status & resolution: Claimed amount ($); the status starts Open. Tap Create claim.
  4. After saving, attach Evidence (photos/documents) and add Follow-up notes (+ Add note) as things happen — status changes are logged automatically.

18Users & Settings

Admins manage accounts in Users; everyone manages their own account in Settings.

HOW TO · Invite a user with the right permissions (admins)

  1. Open Users → + New user.
  2. Enter Email, Full name, and the Role: Member or Admin (full access).
  3. For Members, use the Permissions per section grid: one row per section, three checkboxes — view / edit / create (edit or create implies view). Select all / Clear speed it up.
  4. Tap Send invitation — they get an email to set their password.
  5. From the list you can later Edit permissions, Send reset (password), Deactivate/Activate, and Delete (only inactive users). The table shows each user's Last login.

19Golden rules & glossary

🗑 Deleting a job: possible from the job detail, the Jobs list or Dispatching. It always asks for confirmation, and it removes the job plus its extras and payments and unlinks it from closing sheets. It cannot be undone — prefer Cancelled or On hold when in doubt.

🎯 The 6 rules of the day

1. Check Dispatching — tackle red/orange FADD first.
2. No pickup/delivery today without a driver; chase every 🟥 Not collected tag.
3. Notify drivers with 💬 WhatsApp — the message/manifest is pre-written.
4. Advance the status the moment each stage happens — the board must reflect reality.
5. Watch the Payments red alert (cash 7+ days undeposited) and the In circulation view.
6. Anything broken or missing → open a Claim immediately.

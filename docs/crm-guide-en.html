›Before you start

The essentials for using the system day to day.

This is the system we use to manage pickups, deliveries, storage, trips and collections. What matters:

  • Everything is live: whatever a teammate enters, you see instantly — no page reloads.
  • Permissions: every account has per-section permissions (view / edit / create). If you don't see a module or a button, ask your manager for access.
  • Language: in Settings you can switch the whole app between 🇺🇸 English and 🇪🇸 Español.
  • Start here: with sections 2 (Menu), 3 (Dispatching) and 4 (Jobs) you can already handle 80% of the day.

  • Sub-tabs: All · Pickups today · Deliveries today · In storage · On hold · No trip assigned · No FADD (fill those in!).

4Creating and editing a job

"+ New job" button at the top right of Dispatching, Jobs or Calendar.

Full

Pickup → storage → delivery. Stored in between.

Direct

Pickup → delivery. Straight through, no storage.

Broker delivery

Delivery only: the goods are already here, you just deliver.

The form adapts to the type: it only shows the fields that apply.

What you enter

  • Where it's stored — tick one or more rented units and/or warehouses.
  • Job data — Job #, Client, Type, Status, Broker, Driver (one or several), FADD, Volume (CF) — broker estimate, Real CF (measured at loading), Lot #, Sticker color, Date in.
  • Pickup — date (or date range), address, city, state, zip and balance to collect at pickup.
  • Delivery — date, address, city, state, zip and balance to collect at delivery.
  • Carrier / BOL (broker delivery) — rate per CF, BOL balance, pads.
  • Storage billing — if the client pays monthly for storage (section 8).
📐 Real vs. estimated CF. The broker sends an estimate; when the truck loads, you measure the real volume and enter it in "Real CF (measured at loading)" — in the form, in the job detail, or with the quick "✏️ Real CF" button in lists and trip stops. All occupancy math (units, warehouses and trucks) uses the real CF when it's set; otherwise the estimate. In lists you'll see ✓real or est. Clear the field to fall back to the estimate.

The job detail (tap the Job #)

Almost every field is edited by clicking on it (FADD, client, driver, addresses, balances…). You also get:

  • Top buttons: Edit · 📄 Generate BOL (jumps to BOL with the job preselected) · 💬 WhatsApp · → Advance · 🗑 Delete.
  • Calendar: a banner with the pickup date and buttons to add/edit/remove it from the calendar, plus a manual Calendar status (color): Active / On hold / Cancelled / Long haul / Delivered.
  • Carrier Settlement: the linked closing sheet (click to jump to Settlements), carrier fee, BOL balance, collected amount and Record payment.
  • Extras: the list with amounts and commissions, and + Add extra.
  • Payments: expected / collected / balance, the "On account (unallocated)" money with an Assign button, and + Add payment.

7Chats — the internal messenger 💬

A Messenger-style chat for the team: inbox on the left, conversation on the right.

Conversations

  • Groups (e.g. general, dispatch) — visible to the whole team.
  • Direct messages (DM) — private between two people.
  • New conversation: ✏️ button → search for the person or create a named group.

8Storage Billing — monthly client billing

Some clients pay a monthly fee for storage. The system handles it on its own.

Turn it on in the job

In the form, Storage billing section: tick "Bill this client for storage", enter the monthly rate and choose whether the first month is free. The start date is computed automatically.

Collect and remind

The page shows metrics (pending, overdue, due this week, collected) and tabs (All / Pending / Overdue / Paid). Each 30-day cycle's charge is generated automatically. Per charge: Mark paid and a 💬 Reminder WhatsApp message ready to send.

9Brokers & Clients

🏦 Brokers

List with contact, phone and email. For each one: how many jobs and the total outstanding balance. Add, edit or delete; when creating a job you pick the broker from this list.

👥 Clients

The client list; tap one to see all their jobs.

10Settlements — broker closing sheets

For broker deliveries: each closing sheet groups a broker's jobs and keeps the money math.

  • Metrics and tabs Open / Settled / Disputed / All.
  • Each sheet: included jobs, CF, carrier fee, BOL collected, pads, costs and the net settlement (whether the broker owes you or you owe them).
  • In the detail you record the BOL collection (amount, method, date) — it syncs automatically with Payments: money is never entered twice.
  • Button to export the closing sheet to PDF.
Linking jobs: done from the job detail (Carrier Settlement section), where you can also move the job to another open sheet or a new one.

11Extras & Commissions

Charge and split each job's extras: extra CF, shuttle, long carry, stairs, packing, flight charge, other.

  • Two tabs: 🧑‍✈️ Drivers (grouped by driver) and 👤 Reps / Back office (grouped by rep).
  • Metrics: month's total extras, driver commission (green), rep commission (blue) and what's left for the company (amber).
  • Adding an extra: each job shows the 7 types as rows — tick the box and fill in amount, driver, generated by (Driver only / Driver + Rep / Rep only), rep and commission % (auto-filled, editable).
  • Under each driver: a TOTAL row and a COMMISSION row (yellow, Excel-style), with 📋 Copy and 🖨️ PDF to send each person their summary.
  • Reps / Employees: added with the top button; each rep's profile shows monthly commission and full history.
  • Also: + Add extra from the job detail, or created on the fly when allocating a payment (section 13).

12Trips / Live Load — trips per truck 🛣️

A trip assigns several jobs to one truck and shows the load in real time.

  • Metrics: Active trips · CF in transit · BOL in transit ($ to collect on the road) · Delivered today.
  • Views: 🗺️ Live map · Active trips · Unassigned · All trips. Buttons: ✨ Suggest trips (AI) and + Trip.

Creating a trip

"+ Trip" → automatic number (TRIP-001…), pick the truck, driver and departure date, then search and add jobs. The capacity bar updates live (using real CF when measured) and warns if you go over. New trips start in Loading; status always changes manually: Depart → in transit, Complete trip → completed.

✨ AI suggestions. The Suggest trips (AI) button analyzes the available jobs (where each one really loads from: client, unit or warehouse), the free trucks and the loading trips with room, and proposes new trips and add-ons — each with its occupancy bar and a "💡 Why this trip" box with the reasoning, plus a list of unassigned jobs with the reason. Nothing is created on its own: Review & create / Review & add opens the normal trip modal so you review and confirm.

Each active trip's card

Number (click opens the detail) · status · truck + driver + departure · capacity bar · the list of stops · totals for CF and money to collect. Buttons: Manage Loads, Edit Trip, ➕ Add stop, 🗺️ View route, 💬 Send manifest and Depart.

Stops: jobs + custom stops in one sequence

Job stops

Job, client, route, CF (✓real/est. + ✏️ Real CF button), sticker, lot, FADD and BOL balance. Actions: ✅ Mark delivered, 📦 Drop at storage (in transit) and 🔄 Handoff.

Custom stops (no job)

🔧 Maintenance · 🛠️ Repair · 🔍 DOT inspection · ⚖️ Weigh station · ⛽ Fuel · 🛌 Rest · 🅿️ Parking · 🚚 Equipment · 🏢 Office · 📋 Other. Added with ➕ Add stop (category + optional address and note), edited by tapping them or ✏️, marked done ✅ and removed with ✕.

Reordering: drag by the ⠿ handle — on drop, the whole sequence (jobs + custom) renumbers 1..N.

Manage Loads (the trip detail)

  • ➕ Add job (add an existing job) · 🔼 Load from storage (put a stored job on the truck) · 🆕 Unplanned pickup (create a direct job already on the trip) · 📦 Drop at storage (drop a job at a unit/warehouse mid-trip).
  • After every change, a green banner offers 📲 Send update to the driver via WhatsApp.
  • Trip log: every event on the trip (who and when).
  • When everything is delivered you'll see "✅ All jobs delivered — mark trip as completed?"; if jobs remain when completing, you choose which storage they go to.
🔄 Driver handoff. To pass a job (or the whole trip) to another driver: 🔄 Handoff button → what's handed off, to whom, the reason (better for this delivery / truck change / availability / other) and a note. The new driver becomes the job's primary — cash on hand and commissions follow them. It's logged on the trip log and the stop shows a 🧑‍✈️ tag with their name.

🗺️ Live map

US map with each truck's last known location: green moving, red stopped, grey no data. Truck list with last-update time and an Update location link.

💬 Manifest via WhatsApp

Send manifest to driver builds the full message: trip, truck, driver, departure, total load and % of capacity, total to collect and the numbered stop list with address, FADD, CF, sticker, lot and balance.

13Payments — money, circulation and deposits 💰

Tracks every dollar coming in, who physically holds it and what's been deposited. Buttons: 🏦 Bank accounts and + Payment.

  • Metrics: Expected this month · Received (green) · In circulation / not deposited (red — everything undeposited, any month) · Deposited (blue) · Pending collection (orange) · CC fees (purple).
  • Red alert: payments received but not deposited for 7+ days — job, amount, method, who holds it and for how many days. Those need depositing!
  • Tabs: All / Pending / Received / In circulation / Deposited.
💳 Key rule: digital payments (Zelle, Venmo, card, wire, PayPal, Cash App, Apple Pay) are marked deposited automatically. Cash, checks and money orders stay "in circulation" until you deposit them.

Adding a payment ("+ Payment")

  • Job (search by number or client) · date · amount · concept (Job / Extra / CC Fee / Storage / On account / Other) · discount + reason · stage (At pick up / At delivery / Other).
  • Method: cash, credit card, Zelle, Venmo, check, money order, PayPal, Cash App, wire, Apple Pay. Check: type, serial number (warns on duplicates), bank, date and a photo. Money order: type, serial, office and photo. Credit card: option to charge the CC fee to the client (3% by default) — the fee payment is created automatically.
  • Received (by whom and when). For physical methods: who has the money? (auto-filled with the job's driver) and whether it's been deposited (date + account).
✂️ Allocation per charge ("Assign to charges"). When entering a payment you can split it across the job's charges: the balance and every pending extra, line by line — each shows "Pending: $X" and the remainder after your allocation. Whatever you don't assign stays "On account" (received but not applied); later it's applied with the Assign button. With + New extra / another line you create a new charge on the spot (with its commission).

The "In circulation" view (key for cash flow)

  • Cards per person holding the money: total on hand, breakdown (cash / checks / money orders) and per-job detail with the days held.
  • 💬 Request deposit — a ready-made WhatsApp asking them to deposit.
  • Batch deposit: tick the payments (or Select all), choose a bank account and date → ✓ Deposit N · $total — all marked deposited in one shot.

🏦 Bank accounts

Via Bank accounts you manage accounts (name, bank, type, last 4, active/inactive). Accounts with payments attached can't be deleted — deactivate them instead.

📈 Weekly cash flow

At the bottom: this week's expected, received, in circulation and deposited, plus a projection if everything in circulation gets deposited.

🔄 Sync: recording a collection in Settlements creates/updates the payment in Payments, and vice versa. From the job detail, the Payments section offers "+ Add payment" pre-filled with the balance and the driver.

15BOL — generate and sign 📄

Generates each job's Bill of Lading on top of each company's real PDF, and handles the client's signature via DocuSign.

Templates (set up once per company)

  • + New template → company name → Upload template PDF (the broker/carrier's blank form).
  • ✨ Auto-detect (AI) places the fields automatically (editable), or + Add field to place them by hand: drag and resize each box over the PDF.
  • Each field maps to a job value (client, addresses, dates, charges…), can be fixed or asked at generate time, and there are special types: checkbox, fixed text, named service.
  • Boxes marked as Signature / Initials / Date (for pickup or delivery) become DocuSign signature fields.
  • Save draft or Save & activate — only active templates can be used.

Generating a job's BOL

  • From the section's Generate BOL button, or straight from the job detail (📄 Generate BOL).
  • Split screen: form on the left, live PDF preview on the right. Pick template and job — the data loads automatically, everything editable.
  • Charges calculator: base CF, rate/CF, fuel (% or $), + Extra CF, + Charge, + Discount, template services and checkboxes. Live totals: grand total, deposit, balance due and the due at pickup / due at delivery split (editable).
  • ✎ Add text on PDF: click anywhere on the PDF and type — to add something the template doesn't have, without editing it.
  • ⬇ Download, Save draft or Save as final. Saved BOLs go to Documents, filed under the job's broker.

Client signature ✍️

Generated→ Pickup sent→ Pickup signed→ Delivery sent→ Delivery signed→ Completed ✓
  • Sign pickup — enter the client's email and they get the DocuSign email. The signed copy flows back into the system automatically.
  • Sign delivery — sent over the document that already shows the pickup signature.
  • If you edit a BOL whose pickup is already signed, a new version inheriting the signature is saved (the original stays archived), ready for the delivery signature.
  • Signed copies open via the PU ✍ and DEL ✍ buttons.
📁 Documents: the legal archive of every BOL — folders per broker (+ "All BOLs" and "No broker"), search by client / job / company, and per BOL: View, Reopen (back into the generator), sign and Delete (with confirmation).

16Legal & Compliance 📋

Legal documents for Companies (your LLCs/carriers), Trucks and Drivers, with expiration tracking.

  • Metrics: active companies · expired documents (red) · expiring in 30 days (amber) · up to date (green). The menu's red badge counts what's expired or expiring in ≤7 days, and a red banner lists the urgent items.
  • Tabs: Companies · Trucks · Drivers · All documents.
  • Each company/truck/driver is a card with a cell per document type: Insurance, DOT, MC Authority, IFTA, IRP, W-9… (companies) · Registration, Insurance, annual inspection… (trucks) · CDL, medical card, MVR, drug test… (drivers). Each cell shows the number and "Expires [date] (N days)".
  • Uploading files: drag the photo or PDF straight onto the document's cell (or tap it).
  • + Company and + Document to add; All documents is the full table with filters by entity, status and expiration window (≤7/30/60/90 days).

Automatic statuses

Up to date expires in more than 30 days
Expiring soon expires in ≤30 days
Expired past due
No date expiry not entered yet

Heads up 👀

Trucks and drivers aren't created here (they come from Trucks and from jobs) — here you only manage their documents. Don't let a truck hit the road with expired papers.

19Deleting a job 🗑

You can delete a job from the job detail, the Jobs list or Dispatching (🗑 icon). It always asks for confirmation. Confirming deletes the job and cleans up everything related (extras, payments, unlinks it from the closing sheet).

⚠️ It cannot be undone. Before confirming, make sure it's the right job.

20Golden rules & glossary

🎯 The daily rules

  • Check Dispatching → red/orange FADD first. Make sure no pickup/delivery today is left without a driver.
  • Watch the 🟥 Not collected labels and the red Payments alert (cash undeposited 7+ days).
  • Measure the real CF when the truck loads — units, warehouses and trucks compute occupancy with it.
  • Notify the driver with the 💬 WhatsApp button (job or trip manifest) and advance the status as soon as each stage happens.
  • Generate the BOL from the job and send the pickup signature before loading.
  • If the Compliance badge is red, papers are expired or about to expire.
